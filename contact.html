FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I get my product after purchase? +
After a successful payment, you'll be automatically redirected to a Thank You page with your instant download link. The link is tied to your purchase — no email needed.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We use Razorpay, which supports UPI, debit/credit cards, net banking, and popular wallets. All transactions are encrypted and secure.
Can I get a refund? +
Since our products are digital and delivered instantly, we generally don't offer refunds. However, if you face a genuine issue — like a broken file or wrong product — reach out to us and we'll make it right.
Are the products beginner-friendly? +
Absolutely. Every ASHX product is designed with beginners in mind. Each comes with clear documentation or instructions so you can dive in without prior experience.
Can I use these products commercially? +
Yes! Once you purchase a product, you can use it in your personal and commercial projects. You may not resell the original product files, but the output is yours to use freely.
How often are new products added? +
We add new products regularly based on student demand and trends. Follow us on Instagram or check back on the store page to stay updated.
